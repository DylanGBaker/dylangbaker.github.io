Critical Reflection

When I started on the website, I was excited to learn new languages especially since these are most likely used in everyday life, and I use the internet every day. Since developing my own website I have a newfound respect for people who do this everyday as there as so many decisions and design choices that need to be made. Then implementing the design and bringing it to life is another problem to solve.

Code Design

I am used to coding in languages that are object orientated languages such as C++ and C#. When I started working on HTML and CSS, I tried to use similar concepts to try and make my codes design better and so that other developers find it easier to read. I felt that I have achieved that however, right now I feel that it can change in some areas especially with some of my variable names and in my CSS.

Webpage Design

I like the way that I have structured my webpages but there are some issues. The first issue that I am trying to figure out is the footer. I know you can add the copyright and your name but even when I add that some of the elements on my page still go to the bottom and I am trying to figure out how to get it to stop doing that. I feel that there is an issue with my CSS somewhere and I am debugging it.

The font is something that I am still unsure about as I kind of like it but at the same time while developing the website and seeing the font a lot I would like to change it but before I do, I would like to get more input from other people.

I am a person that really enjoys and believes in the iterative design process. But in that process, I get the main choices and ideas out first before I start to make it pretty. I would rather have something that is usable and lacks some CSS than have a lot of CSS to make it look nice but then nothing works. For now, I have not deemed the about me section that important yet or the contact section. I am mainly focusing on the home page, as that is what people normally see first, and the blog section. I am still currently testing stuff on the home page and so if the wireframe is not the same that is why.

Conclusion

Throughout the process of designing and implementing this website has been very enjoyable to sit down and work on placement of elements and seeing what works best in my mind at that time but, it definitely has had its downs. I am not the best at art and so picking the correct colour for elements has been a trouble for me. But I am getting there as well as growing as a developer and designer. This is definitely one of the projects I have enjoyed the most. So far at least.
